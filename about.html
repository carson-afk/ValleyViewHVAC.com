Our story

A Salem HVAC partner
built for the long run.

Founded in 2019, Valley View HVAC is led by technicians with 20+ years combined experience in Willamette Valley homes — because Salem's climate demands different thinking than what you'll find in a textbook.

Climate-aware design,
neighborhood-level service.

Salem is not Dallas. Not Denver. Not the textbook "mild climate." Our winters are long, wet, and occasionally bitter — and our summer heat waves now run longer and hotter than they used to.

Valley View designs systems for this climate: right-sized heat pumps with cold-climate performance, humidity-managing ductless setups, and indoor air quality solutions tuned for Willamette valley wildfire smoke season.
