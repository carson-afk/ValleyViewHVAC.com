Everything you should know about affiliate services in Salem.

Why we refer instead of subcontract

When a project needs work outside HVAC, you want a specialist — not a jack-of-all-trades. We keep a short list of contractors we've worked alongside for years and trust to do right by our customers.

We don't take referral fees

Our recommendations are based on quality, not kickbacks. If a partner ever disappoints a customer, they come off the list.

Coordinated scheduling

For bigger projects we'll coordinate timing between contractors so you're not playing project manager between five different companies.

Questions, answered

FAQ — Affiliate Services.

Do you charge for referrals?

No. We don't take kickbacks from our partners — referrals are free to you.

What if a referral doesn't work out?

Tell us. Partners come off the list if they disappoint our customers. We'd rather lose a referral relationship than damage trust with you.

Can you coordinate multiple trades?

For larger projects (HVAC + electrical panel upgrade + new service, for example), yes. We scope timing across contractors so you get one completion date instead of five.
